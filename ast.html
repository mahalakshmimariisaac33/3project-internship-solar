C-type (Carbonaceous)

Dark and rich in carbon, these make up over 75% of known asteroids and are believed to contain water.

S-type (Silicaceous)

Made of silicate and nickel-iron, S-type asteroids are found mostly in the inner asteroid belt.

M-type (Metallic)

These metallic asteroids contain nickel and iron and may be the remains of asteroid cores.
